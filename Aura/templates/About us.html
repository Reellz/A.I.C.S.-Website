Problem Statement

In today's globalized society, many families struggle to find an educational institution that aligns with their values while providing rigorous academic programs. Public and conventional private schools often fail to incorporate spiritual growth and moral education, resulting in students who may excel academically but lack a strong ethical foundation.

Solution

Aura International Christian School offers a curriculum that integrates academic rigor with spiritual and moral education. By employing qualified educators who are committed to both academic excellence and Christian values, we are able to create a nurturing environment where students can thrive intellectually and spiritually. Our innovative programs and diverse extracurricular activities, conducted in a homely environment, further enhance the educational experience, ensuring that our students are well-rounded individuals.

Objectives

  1. Provide quality education through Early Childhood Development and the ACE Curriculum (4-18+ years).
  2. Cultivate godliness through discipleship and mentorship.
  3. Develop innovative solutions through research.
  4. Empower parents to reinforce godly values.
  5. Helping working parents with baby care.

Services

  • Early Childhood Development (ECD) and day care services.
  • ACE program (Pre-school, primary, secondary, A-levels).
  • Home Schoolers support.
  • Community outreach programs.

About AICS

AICS uses the Accelerated Christian Education(ACE) curriculum. At AICS, we provide a Christ-centered education using ACE which is an individualized, self-paced, and bible-based programme.
