Welcome to Aura International Christian School!

Growing to glow

Aura International Christian School is positioned to become a leading educational institution that blends innovative and academic excellence with Christian values. Located in a diverse and multicultural environment, the school aims to provide an exceptional learning experience for students of various backgrounds while nurturing their faith. With a commitment to holistic education, we strive to empower students to reach their full potential spiritually, academically, and socially.

Explore A.I.C.S.

A.I.C.S is an institution that aims to prepare Godly leaders, Focused innovators, and Responsible members of society. In a world that is advancing so quickly and is introducing people to its new set standards, some of which are having a negative impact in society and are derailing the society from the Lord and the purpose for which man was created. We aim to achieve these goals through spiritual guidance of students, daily devotion, and discipleship using the ACE Curriculum.

About AICS

AICS uses the Accelerated Christian Education(ACE) curriculum. At AICS, we provide a Christ-centered education using ACE which is an individualized, self-paced, and bible-based programme.
